## Observations 

1. the precision trend is identical to [EXP_002](EXP_002.md) 
2. the sensitivity drop is not as aggressive as [EXP_002](EXP_002.md) - meaning the model is less aggresively ignoring apnea
3. the TP increased from 91 to 169 and based on the above observation
4. macro F1 score is also following a similar behavior

## Hypothesis

1. the near normal patients during training is the leading hypo
2. high variability


## Interpretation and Conclusion

this exp showed that reducing channels helped model surface more apnea events but the overall results are still similar and now the primary hypotehsis to test out is stratifying the train test split by removing the patients below 5% apnea
